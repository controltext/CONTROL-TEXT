NOTES:  
  
Attention is finite. Every act of attention simultaneously emphasizes certain signals while excluding others. What receives attention gains influence over perception, memory, and behavior.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Reality is filtered through attention before it is interpreted through language. Attention functions as a reality-selection mechanism that transforms an infinite field of possible information into a finite field of conscious experience. Through attention, symbols gain significance, narratives gain influence, beliefs gain reinforcement, and identities gain stability. Control Text emerges when the allocation of attention determines the boundaries of perceived reality itself.
