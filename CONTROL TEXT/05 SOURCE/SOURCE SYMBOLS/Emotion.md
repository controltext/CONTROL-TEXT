NOTES:  
  
Emotion is not merely a reaction to reality. Emotion helps determine which aspects of reality become memorable, meaningful, believable, and actionable.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Emotion functions as a significance-allocation mechanism that transforms information into lived experience. Through emotion, symbols acquire power, narratives acquire urgency, beliefs acquire conviction, and identities acquire attachment. Control Text emerges when emotional significance determines which realities are reinforced, remembered, defended, and transmitted. What moves emotion often moves behavior, regardless of objective accuracy.
