CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Reality functions as the experiential field upon which all symbolic systems operate. Through reality, information becomes observable, attention becomes selective, language becomes meaningful, narratives become believable, identities become inhabitable, and power becomes consequential. Control Text emerges when symbolic systems shape not merely the interpretation of reality, but the experience of reality itself. The struggle for control is often a struggle over which version of reality becomes dominant, accepted, and enacted.
