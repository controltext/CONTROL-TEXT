NOTES:  
  
Narratives do not simply describe events. They organize events into meaningful structures by assigning causes, motivations, identities, values, relationships, and significance.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Human beings rarely experience raw information. A narrative functions as a meaning-organization mechanism that transforms symbols into reality frameworks. Through narratives, experiences acquire interpretation, identities acquire purpose, histories acquire coherence, and futures acquire direction. Control Text emerges when narratives become the primary structures through which reality is perceived, remembered, and acted upon.
