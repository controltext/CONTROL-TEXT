SYSTEM [[13 TIME CONSTRUCTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A chronological arrangement of events, periods, actions, developments, or milestones organized according to their position in time.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Provides a framework for understanding temporal relationships by placing events into an ordered sequence that establishes continuity, progression, causation, and historical context.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Historical timelines  
- Project timelines  
- Personal life timelines  
- Organizational histories  
- Development roadmaps  
- Event sequences  
- Chronological records

NOTES:  
  
Timelines organize events into sequences that make patterns, relationships, developments, and changes easier to perceive and communicate. Different timelines may emphasize different events, starting points, or interpretations.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Events gain meaning when they are placed within a temporal sequence. Timelines transform isolated occurrences into structured narratives by establishing order, continuity, progression, and perceived relationships between past, present, and future.
