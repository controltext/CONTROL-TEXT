SYSTEM [[07 SEMANTIC WARFARE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The gradual or deliberate replacement, modification, redefinition, or evolution of words, phrases, labels, and linguistic conventions within a language system.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Changes perception and interpretation by altering the vocabulary used to describe people, events, ideas, institutions, and reality itself.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- "Global Warming" → "Climate Change"  
- "Propaganda" → "Public Relations"  
- "Secretary" → "Administrative Assistant"  
- "Mental Retardation" → "Intellectual Disability"  
- "War Department" → "Department of Defense"  
- Brand renaming campaigns  
- Political terminology shifts

NOTES:  
  
Terminology shifts may emerge through cultural change, institutional policy, political influence, technological development, social movements, or strategic communication efforts. The shift itself can alter interpretation even when the underlying subject remains unchanged.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Changes in terminology often signal deeper changes in perception, values, identity, or power. When language evolves, the conceptual framework through which reality is understood may evolve with it.
