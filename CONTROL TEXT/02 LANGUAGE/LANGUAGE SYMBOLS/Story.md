SYSTEM [[17 LANGUAGE ENGINE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A structured sequence of events, experiences, characters, actions, ideas, or meanings communicated through language in a narrative form.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Organizes information and meaning into coherent patterns that can be communicated, remembered, interpreted, transmitted, and understood across individuals and groups.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Personal stories  
- Historical narratives  
- Myths  
- Novels  
- Folktales  
- Case studies  
- Anecdotes

NOTES:  
  
Stories provide a framework through which information can be organized into sequences involving causes, effects, motivations, developments, conflicts, and resolutions. They may communicate facts, values, identities, lessons, beliefs, or entertainment.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Language becomes more memorable when it is organized into narrative structures. Stories function as meaning containers that transform isolated facts, events, and ideas into coherent sequences, allowing information to be more easily understood, remembered, transmitted, and integrated into collective understanding.
