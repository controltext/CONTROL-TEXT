SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The act of updating, reloading, or revisiting a stream of information in order to access newly available content, signals, events, or communications.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Encourages repeated attention allocation by creating opportunities for novelty, discovery, engagement, and ongoing information consumption.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Pull-to-refresh on social media  
- Refreshing a news feed  
- Checking notifications repeatedly  
- Reloading message boards  
- Updating search results  
- Monitoring live feeds  
- Revisiting content platforms

NOTES:  
  
Feed refresh behavior is often driven by uncertainty regarding what new information may appear. The possibility of discovering something valuable, entertaining, socially relevant, or surprising can encourage repeated engagement.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The expectation of novelty can become a powerful attention driver. When information streams are continuously updated, individuals may repeatedly return to them in anticipation of new content, creating recurring cycles of attention and engagement.
