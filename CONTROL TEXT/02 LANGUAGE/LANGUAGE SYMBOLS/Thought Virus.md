SYSTEM [[11 MEMETIC ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
An idea, belief, narrative, concept, or pattern of thought that spreads from person to person through communication, repetition, imitation, and social transmission.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Facilitates the propagation of ideas by encouraging continued replication, discussion, adoption, and dissemination across individuals and groups.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Viral beliefs  
- Conspiracy narratives  
- Popular slogans  
- Cultural catchphrases  
- Ideological concepts  
- Internet phenomena  
- Self-replicating narratives

NOTES:  
  
Thought viruses spread through communication networks in a manner similar to other cultural information. Their persistence often depends upon memorability, emotional impact, relevance, repetition, and ease of transmission.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Ideas can replicate by moving from one mind to another through language, symbols, stories, and social interaction. The more easily an idea can be remembered, repeated, and shared, the greater its potential for memetic persistence and distribution.
