DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The transmission, exchange, interpretation, and reception of information, meaning, ideas, intentions, emotions, instructions, symbols, or messages between individuals, groups, systems, or entities.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Enables the transfer of meaning by connecting senders and receivers through shared symbols, language structures, signals, or communication frameworks.
