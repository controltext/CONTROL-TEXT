SYSTEM [[13 TIME CONSTRUCTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A designated point in time by which an action, task, obligation, event, or objective is expected, required, or intended to be completed.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Organizes behavior by establishing temporal boundaries that influence planning, prioritization, coordination, and decision-making.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Project deadlines  
- Work assignments  
- Tax filing dates  
- Application deadlines  
- Contract completion dates  
- Academic due dates  
- Regulatory deadlines

NOTES:  
  
Deadlines create temporal expectations by linking actions to specific points in time. They are commonly used to coordinate activities, allocate resources, establish accountability, and synchronize collective behavior.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Time gains behavioral influence when it is attached to expectations. A deadline converts an abstract measurement of time into a practical boundary that shapes attention, urgency, planning, and action.
