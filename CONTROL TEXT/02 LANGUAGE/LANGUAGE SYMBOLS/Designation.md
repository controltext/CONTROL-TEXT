SYSTEMS [[15 THE NAME MACHINE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A formally assigned name, title, label, classification, status, role, or identifier used to recognize, distinguish, categorize, or define an individual, group, object, position, or entity.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms classifications into recognized identities by assigning official names, titles, categories, or statuses within a structured system.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Job titles  
- Official classifications  
- Professional credentials  
- Military designations  
- Product designations  
- Government classifications  
- Organizational roles
