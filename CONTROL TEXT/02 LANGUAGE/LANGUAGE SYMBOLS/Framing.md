SYSTEM [[07 SEMANTIC WARFARE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The presentation of information, concepts, events, or experiences within a particular interpretive context that influences how they are understood.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Directs perception by establishing the perspective, assumptions, boundaries, and meanings through which information is interpreted.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- "Tax Relief" vs "Public Investment"  
- "Freedom Fighter" vs "Insurgent"  
- "Risk" vs "Opportunity"  
- "Security Measure" vs "Restriction"  
- "Climate Crisis" vs "Climate Change"  
- "Investment" vs "Expense"

NOTES:  
  
Framing influences interpretation by determining the conceptual lens through which information is viewed. Different frames can produce different conclusions even when the underlying facts remain unchanged.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People rarely interact with information directly. Information is almost always encountered through frames that shape what is noticed, emphasized, questioned, or ignored.
