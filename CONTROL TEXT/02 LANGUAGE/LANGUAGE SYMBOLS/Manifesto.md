NOTES:  
  
Manifestos are often created during periods of formation, transformation, reform, or movement-building. They serve to define identity, communicate purpose, establish principles, and inspire coordinated action.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Texts do not merely preserve existing systems; they can also create new ones. A manifesto functions as a declaration of intent that transforms ideas, values, and aspirations into a durable textual form capable of organizing identity, motivating participation, and guiding future action.
