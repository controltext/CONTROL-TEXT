SYSTEM [[11 MEMETIC ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A behavior, idea, phrase, symbol, challenge, style, narrative, or piece of content that rapidly spreads through a population via repeated sharing, imitation, and participation.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Accelerates the transmission of information and cultural patterns by encouraging widespread replication across individuals, groups, and communication networks.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Viral hashtags  
- Internet challenges  
- Popular dances  
- Viral videos  
- Catchphrases  
- Fashion trends  
- Social media phenomena

NOTES:  
  
Viral trends spread through visibility, repetition, imitation, social participation, and network effects. Their growth often depends upon ease of replication, emotional resonance, novelty, and audience engagement.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Ideas become powerful when they are repeatedly reproduced by others. Viral trends transform individual actions into collective behaviors by encouraging widespread imitation and participation across social networks.
