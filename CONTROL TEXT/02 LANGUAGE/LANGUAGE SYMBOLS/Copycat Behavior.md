SYSTEM [[11 MEMETIC ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The replication of behaviors, actions, expressions, ideas, practices, or patterns observed in others.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Facilitates memetic transmission by allowing behaviors, ideas, and cultural patterns to spread through observation, imitation, and repetition.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Viral challenges  
- Social media trends  
- Fashion trends  
- Popular phrases  
- Behavioral imitation  
- Influencer mimicry  
- Group participation behaviors

NOTES:  
  
Copycat behavior allows ideas and actions to spread without requiring direct instruction. The visibility of a behavior often increases the likelihood that others will adopt and reproduce it.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Many cultural patterns spread through imitation rather than persuasion. When individuals observe a behavior being repeated by others, the behavior becomes easier to recognize, reproduce, and transmit, increasing its memetic survivability within a population.
