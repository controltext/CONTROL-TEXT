SYSTEM [[10 CONSENSUS REALITY]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A symbolic medium of exchange that derives value from collective recognition, acceptance, and trust within a group, society, or economic system.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Facilitates exchange, measurement, storage, and communication of value by providing a shared symbolic standard recognized by participants.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- National currencies  
- Coins  
- Paper money  
- Digital currencies  
- Bank balances  
- Commodity-backed currencies  
- Local exchange systems

NOTES:  
  
Currency functions because participants collectively recognize and accept its value. While currencies may be backed by resources, institutions, legal systems, or economic activity, their effectiveness ultimately depends upon shared belief and participation.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Currency demonstrates how collective belief can create functional reality. The value of a monetary symbol emerges not from the physical object itself, but from widespread agreement regarding what it represents and what it can be exchanged for.
