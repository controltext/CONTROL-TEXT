SYSTEM [[10 CONSENSUS REALITY]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A belief, viewpoint, interpretation, preference, or judgment perceived to be held by the largest portion of a group, population, or community.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Influences perception, behavior, and decision-making by establishing a reference point for what is considered normal, accepted, popular, or legitimate.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Public opinion polls  
- Social norms  
- Election outcomes  
- Cultural attitudes  
- Popular trends  
- Community consensus  
- Mainstream viewpoints

NOTES:  
  
Majority opinion may be real, perceived, measured, assumed, or constructed. Individuals frequently adjust beliefs and behaviors based on what they believe the majority thinks, regardless of whether that perception is accurate.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People often use the perceived beliefs of others as a shortcut for determining what is true, acceptable, safe, or legitimate. The appearance of widespread agreement can influence behavior even when the underlying consensus is uncertain or incomplete.
