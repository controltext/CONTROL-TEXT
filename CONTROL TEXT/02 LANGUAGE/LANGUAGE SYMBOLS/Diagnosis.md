SYSTEMS [[15 THE NAME MACHINE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The process of identifying, classifying, labeling, or categorizing a condition, pattern, phenomenon, situation, or state based upon observed characteristics, evidence, criteria, or interpretation.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms observations into recognizable categories by assigning names and classifications that allow communication, understanding, decision-making, and action.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Medical diagnoses  
- Technical diagnoses  
- Psychological diagnoses  
- Organizational assessments  
- Problem identification  
- System evaluations  
- Fault detection

CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The act of naming often begins with classification. Diagnosis transforms uncertainty into a recognized category by assigning a label to a collection of observations, allowing a phenomenon to become identifiable, discussable, and actionable within a system of meaning.
