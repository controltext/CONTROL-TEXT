SYSTEM [[12 IDENTITY PROGRAMMING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A role, occupation, trade, vocation, or field of work through which an individual is identified, recognized, and socially categorized.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Provides a framework for identity by associating individuals with specific roles, skills, responsibilities, expectations, and social meanings.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Teacher  
- Artist  
- Doctor  
- Engineer  
- Soldier  
- Entrepreneur  
- Carpenter

NOTES:  
  
Professions often extend beyond economic activity and become components of personal and social identity. Individuals may adopt values, behaviors, communities, expectations, and reputations associated with their profession.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People are frequently identified through what they do. Over time, occupational roles can become integrated into self-concept, influencing how individuals perceive themselves and how they are perceived by others.
