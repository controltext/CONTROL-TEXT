SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A subject, event, phrase, idea, or piece of information that experiences a significant increase in public attention, discussion, visibility, or engagement within a population or platform.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Directs collective attention toward specific topics by increasing their visibility, perceived relevance, and social prominence.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Trending hashtags  
- Viral news stories  
- Popular search terms  
- Social media trends

NOTES:  
  
Trending topics often emerge through concentrated attention, algorithmic amplification, social sharing, media coverage, or collective interest. Visibility itself can attract additional attention, creating self-reinforcing feedback loops.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention frequently follows attention. When a topic is perceived as important, popular, or widely discussed, individuals often become more likely to notice, investigate, discuss, and share it, further increasing its visibility.
