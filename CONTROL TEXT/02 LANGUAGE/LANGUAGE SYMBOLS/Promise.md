SYSTEM [[16 THE SPELL FACTORY]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A statement through which an individual, group, or entity commits to a future action, behavior, outcome, obligation, or intention.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Uses language to create expectations about future conduct, establishing a verbal commitment that influences trust, planning, cooperation, and decision-making.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Personal promises  
- Political promises  
- Business commitments  
- Relationship commitments  
- Public pledges  
- Organizational commitments  
- Verbal assurances

NOTES:  
  
Promises connect language to future behavior. Their influence often depends upon credibility, trust, reputation, accountability, social expectations, and the perceived ability or willingness of the speaker to fulfill the commitment.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Some forms of language attempt to shape the future rather than describe the present. A promise creates an expectation that a future action will occur, transforming words into commitments that influence planning, trust, cooperation, and anticipated outcomes.
