SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A perceived gap between what an individual knows and what they want to know, creating a desire to seek additional information.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Captures and sustains attention by generating uncertainty, anticipation, or incomplete understanding that motivates further engagement.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- "You Won't Believe What Happened Next"  
- Cliffhangers  
- Mystery narratives  
- Teaser trailers  
- Unanswered questions  
- Partial information reveals  
- Suspense-driven content

NOTES:  
  
Curiosity gaps function by creating a sense of incompleteness. The greater the perceived value of the missing information, the stronger the motivation to continue paying attention or seeking resolution.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention is often drawn not only toward information itself, but toward the absence of information. Unanswered questions, incomplete narratives, and unresolved uncertainty can become powerful drivers of engagement and continued focus.
