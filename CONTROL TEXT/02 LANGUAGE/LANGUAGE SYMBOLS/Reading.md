SYSTEM [[17 LANGUAGE ENGINE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The process of interpreting, decoding, understanding, and extracting meaning from written symbols, words, sentences, texts, or other recorded forms of language.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms written symbols into meaning by allowing information, ideas, instructions, narratives, knowledge, and communication to be received and interpreted.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Reading books  
- Reading articles  
- Reading documents  
- Reading messages  
- Reading instructions  
- Reading signs  
- Reading digital content

NOTES:  
  
Reading involves recognizing symbols, interpreting language structures, accessing definitions, understanding context, and constructing meaning from recorded communication. Different readers may derive different interpretations depending on knowledge, experience, context, and perspective.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Writing encodes meaning into symbols. Reading decodes symbols back into meaning. Reading functions as the interpretation layer of the language engine, allowing information stored in text to be reconstructed within the mind of a reader long after the original communication occurred.
