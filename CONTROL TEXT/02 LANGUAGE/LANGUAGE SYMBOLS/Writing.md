SYSTEM [[17 LANGUAGE ENGINE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The act or process of recording language, meaning, information, ideas, instructions, narratives, or symbols into a durable visual form through the use of a writing system.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms spoken, imagined, or conceptual language into persistent symbolic structures that can be preserved, transmitted, copied, studied, and interpreted across time and space.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Handwriting  
- Typing  
- Literature  
- Documentation  
- Journaling  
- Record keeping  
- Text creation

NOTES:  
  
Writing allows language to persist beyond the moment of speech. Through writing, ideas, instructions, histories, stories, laws, agreements, and knowledge can be stored, replicated, distributed, and transmitted across generations.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Language becomes durable when it is written. Writing functions as the preservation layer of the language engine, converting transient communication into persistent symbolic artifacts that can survive time, distance, memory limitations, and the lifespan of individual speakers.
