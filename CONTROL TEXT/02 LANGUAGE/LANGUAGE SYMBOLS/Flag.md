DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A symbolic object used to represent an identity, group, nation, movement, organization, belief system, or collective affiliation.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Condenses complex ideas, histories, loyalties, and identities into a visible symbol that can be recognized, displayed, defended, and emotionally invested in.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- National flags  
- Military flags  
- Religious banners  
- Political movement flags  
- Corporate logos used as flags  
- Pride flags  
- Sports team flags

NOTES:  
  
Flags function as symbolic containers. Individuals often associate them with shared identity, values, history, territory, beliefs, or collective purpose. Reactions to flags frequently extend beyond the physical object itself to the meanings attached to it.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The emotional significance of a flag rarely comes from the material object. Its influence emerges from the collective meanings, memories, identities, and loyalties projected onto the symbol.
