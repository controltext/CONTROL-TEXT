SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A message, event, image, statement, or piece of content designed to provoke anger, indignation, moral judgment, offense, or emotional reaction in order to attract and sustain attention.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Captures and amplifies attention by activating emotional responses that encourage engagement, discussion, sharing, criticism, or conflict.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Controversial statements  
- Provocative headlines  
- Public scandals  
- Political controversies  
- Rage-bait content  
- Moral outrage campaigns  
- Social media conflicts

NOTES:  
  
Outrage triggers operate by activating emotional investment and perceived significance. Content that provokes anger, offense, injustice, or conflict often receives increased attention, discussion, and distribution regardless of whether audiences agree or disagree with it.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention frequently follows emotional intensity. Outrage can transform passive observers into active participants by encouraging reaction, commentary, sharing, opposition, or defense. The stronger the emotional response, the greater the likelihood that attention will persist and spread.
