SYSTEM [[17 LANGUAGE ENGINE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The significance, interpretation, understanding, concept, association, or informational content conveyed, represented, or perceived through symbols, language, experiences, events, or communication.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Provides the interpretive substance that allows symbols, words, sentences, stories, and communications to convey understanding rather than remain merely collections of signs.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Word meanings  
- Symbolic meanings  
- Personal interpretations  
- Cultural meanings  
- Narrative meanings  
- Conceptual understandings  
- Contextual significance

NOTES:  
  
Meaning may emerge through definitions, context, relationships, interpretation, experience, culture, symbolism, and communication. The same symbol or statement may carry different meanings depending on perspective, context, and framework.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Language is not an end in itself. Meaning functions as the informational layer that language attempts to carry. Symbols, words, grammar, stories, and communication systems serve as vehicles through which meaning is encoded, transmitted, interpreted, transformed, and reconstructed across minds and generations.
