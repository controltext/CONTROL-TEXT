SYSTEM [[08 SYMBOLIC POSSESSSION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

  
DEFINITION:  
  
A standardized form of dress used to visually represent membership, identity, role, status, authority, affiliation, or participation within a group, institution, or system.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Communicates symbolic identity by making group membership, social roles, authority structures, and collective affiliations immediately recognizable.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Military uniforms  
- Police uniforms  
- Religious garments  
- School uniforms  
- Corporate attire  
- Sports team uniforms  
- Organizational dress codes

NOTES:  
  
Uniforms function as wearable symbols that visually communicate identity and affiliation. Their significance emerges not from the clothing itself, but from the meanings, expectations, authority, and social recognition attached to it.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
A uniform transforms abstract membership into visible form. Through repeated association, uniforms often acquire symbolic power, allowing observers to immediately recognize authority, belonging, status, expertise, or allegiance based on appearance alone.
