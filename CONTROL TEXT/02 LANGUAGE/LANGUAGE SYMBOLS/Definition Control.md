SYSTEM [[07 SEMANTIC WARFARE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The process of influencing, establishing, modifying, or controlling the meaning assigned to words, symbols, labels, concepts, and terminology.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Shapes perception and interpretation by determining how symbols are understood within a language system.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Redefinition of political terms  
- Brand messaging  
- Dictionary revisions  
- Ideological language shifts  
- Corporate terminology  
- Legal definitions  
- Social movement slogans

NOTES:  
  
Control over definitions can influence how events, behaviors, identities, and institutions are perceived. Changes in language often precede changes in social, political, or cultural understanding.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The power to define often becomes the power to frame reality. When the meaning of a symbol changes, the interpretation of everything connected to that symbol may change as well.
