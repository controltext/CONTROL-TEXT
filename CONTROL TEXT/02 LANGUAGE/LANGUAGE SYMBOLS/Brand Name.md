SYSTEM [[16 THE SPELL FACTORY]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A deliberately chosen name used to identify, distinguish, represent, and communicate the identity of a product, service, organization, individual, movement, or entity.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Uses language to create recognition, association, memory, meaning, trust, identity, and emotional connection around a target.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Corporate brands  
- Product names  
- Service brands  
- Artist names  
- Business names  
- Organization names  
- Movement names

NOTES:  
  
Brand names often function as condensed symbolic containers that accumulate associations, narratives, emotions, experiences, expectations, reputation, and recognition over time. Their effectiveness frequently depends upon memorability, repetition, differentiation, and meaning.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Names can influence perception before any direct experience occurs. A brand name functions as a symbolic trigger that concentrates identity, reputation, expectation, and narrative into a single linguistic form, allowing meaning to be rapidly communicated and recalled through repeated exposure.
