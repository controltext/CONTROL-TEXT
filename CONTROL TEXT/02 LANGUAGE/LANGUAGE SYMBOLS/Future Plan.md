SYSTEM [[13 TIME CONSTRUCTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A structured intention, strategy, objective, or course of action organized around anticipated future events, conditions, goals, or outcomes.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms the future from an abstract possibility into an organized framework for decision-making, preparation, coordination, and action.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Business plans  
- Personal goals  
- Strategic roadmaps  
- Retirement plans  
- Project plans  
- Development plans  
- Life plans

NOTES:  
  
Future plans organize anticipated actions and objectives across time. They may be short-term or long-term, individual or collective, flexible or fixed, and are often influenced by forecasts, priorities, resources, and desired outcomes.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The future becomes actionable when it is structured. Future plans transform uncertain possibilities into organized pathways, allowing individuals and groups to coordinate present behavior around anticipated outcomes that do not yet exist.
