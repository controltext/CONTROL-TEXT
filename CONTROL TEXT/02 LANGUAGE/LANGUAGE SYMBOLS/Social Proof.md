SYSTEM [[11 MEMETIC ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The tendency to interpret the popularity, adoption, repetition, or endorsement of an idea, behavior, belief, or practice as evidence of its validity, desirability, or importance.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Accelerates memetic transmission by encouraging individuals to adopt ideas and behaviors that appear widely accepted, supported, or practiced by others.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- High view counts  
- Large follower counts  
- Viral content  
- Product reviews  
- Public endorsements  
- Crowd participation  
- Trending content

NOTES:  
  
Social proof often functions as a shortcut for evaluating ideas, behaviors, products, and beliefs. Visible adoption can increase the likelihood that additional individuals will participate, creating self-reinforcing cycles of replication.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People frequently use the behavior of others as a signal for what deserves attention, trust, acceptance, or imitation. The appearance of widespread adoption can increase the perceived legitimacy and transmissibility of a meme regardless of its intrinsic qualities.
