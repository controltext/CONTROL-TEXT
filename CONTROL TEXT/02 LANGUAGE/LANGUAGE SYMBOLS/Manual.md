NOTES:  
  
Manuals focus on practical application rather than theory alone. They allow knowledge, procedures, standards, and methods to be preserved and reproduced consistently across individuals, organizations, and generations.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Knowledge becomes operational when it is translated into procedure. Manuals transform abstract concepts into actionable instructions, allowing skills, systems, and processes to be replicated reliably across time and participants.
