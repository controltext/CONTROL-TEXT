DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The deliberate introduction and repetition of specific words, phrases, terms, or concepts into communication in order to influence perception, association, interpretation, or discourse.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Shapes understanding by increasing the visibility, frequency, and cognitive availability of selected concepts within a language environment.

- Political talking points  
- Marketing buzzwords  
- Campaign messaging  
- Hashtag campaigns  
- Corporate terminology

NOTES:  
  
Keyword injection influences discourse by increasing the frequency and prominence of specific language patterns. Repeated exposure can strengthen familiarity, association, and the perceived relevance of selected concepts.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The words that appear most often within a communication environment frequently shape what people discuss, remember, search for, and associate with reality. Repetition can increase the perceived significance of a concept regardless of its objective importance.
