SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A title, phrase, or opening statement designed to summarize, introduce, and attract attention toward a piece of information, content, event, or narrative.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Acts as an attention gateway by influencing whether information is noticed, ignored, investigated, or shared.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- News headlines  
- Article titles  
- YouTube video titles  
- Blog post titles  
- Social media post headlines  
- Newspaper front-page titles  
- Marketing copy

NOTES:  
  
Headlines often function as condensed narratives that influence first impressions before deeper engagement occurs. The language, framing, emotional tone, and wording of a headline can significantly affect interpretation and attention.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
For many people, the headline becomes the message. Initial framing frequently shapes perception before additional context or information is encountered.
