DEFINITION:  
  
The enduring impact, influence, memory, work, values, achievements, or consequences that persist beyond an individual's, group's, institution's, or civilization's immediate existence.

NOTES:  
  
Legacies are formed through the accumulation of actions, ideas, relationships, achievements, institutions, narratives, and cultural contributions. Their significance often depends upon how they are remembered, preserved, interpreted, and transmitted across generations.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Time construction is not limited to the measurement of moments or the recording of events. Legacy extends the influence of individuals and groups beyond their immediate lifespan by transforming actions into memories, memories into history, and history into enduring continuity across time.
