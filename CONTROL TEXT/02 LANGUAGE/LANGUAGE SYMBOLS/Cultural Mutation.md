SYSTEM [[11 MEMETIC ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The modification, adaptation, reinterpretation, or transformation of ideas, beliefs, narratives, symbols, behaviors, or cultural patterns as they are transmitted through a population.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Generates variation within memetic systems by altering existing cultural information, allowing ideas to evolve, adapt, and persist within changing environments.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Evolving internet memes  
- Changing traditions  
- Adapted stories  
- Modified slogans  
- Reinterpreted symbols  
- Evolving ideologies  
- Cultural remixing

NOTES:  
  
As ideas move between individuals, groups, generations, and cultures, they are often altered through reinterpretation, simplification, elaboration, recombination, or contextual adaptation. These changes can affect how effectively a meme spreads and survives.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Transmission rarely produces perfect copies. Cultural information often changes as it spreads, creating new variations that compete for attention, adoption, and persistence. Memetic systems evolve through the continuous interaction of replication and modification.
