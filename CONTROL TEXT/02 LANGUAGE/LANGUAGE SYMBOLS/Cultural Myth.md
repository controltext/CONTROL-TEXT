SYSTEM [[10 CONSENSUS REALITY]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A widely shared story, narrative, belief, or symbolic interpretation that helps a group explain, understand, justify, or reinforce aspects of its identity, values, history, or worldview.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Provides a shared framework through which groups interpret reality, establish meaning, transmit values, and maintain collective identity across time.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Founding stories  
- National origin narratives  
- Heroic legends  
- Cultural folklore  
- Collective historical narratives  
- Social myths  
- Civilizational stories

NOTES:  
  
Cultural myths may contain historical, symbolic, moral, or allegorical elements. Their influence often comes less from factual accuracy and more from their ability to organize meaning, reinforce identity, and provide shared interpretations of reality.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Groups frequently organize collective understanding through stories. Over time, repeated narratives can become embedded within culture, shaping identity, values, expectations, and perceptions of reality regardless of their historical origins.
