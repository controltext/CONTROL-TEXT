DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A word, category, designation, or identifier used to classify, describe, distinguish, or assign identity to an individual, group, concept, behavior, or entity.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Organizes identity by placing people, groups, and ideas into recognizable categories that influence perception, expectation, interpretation, and social interaction.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Personal labels  
- Social categories  
- Occupational titles  
- Political labels  
- Personality labels  
- Cultural classifications  
- Group identifiers

NOTES:  
  
Labels simplify complex identities into recognizable categories. They can assist communication and organization, but may also influence expectations, assumptions, perceptions, and self-concept.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Labels function as identity containers. Once a label is applied, individuals and groups are often interpreted through the characteristics, assumptions, narratives, and expectations associated with that category. The label can become a lens through which identity is perceived by both others and oneself.
