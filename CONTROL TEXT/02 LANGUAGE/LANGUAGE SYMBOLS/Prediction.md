NOTES:  
  
Predictions attempt to extend understanding beyond the present by using information from past and current conditions to anticipate future outcomes. Their accuracy may vary depending upon uncertainty, available information, assumptions, and changing circumstances.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The future does not yet exist as an observable reality. Predictions function as temporal models that allow individuals and groups to imagine, evaluate, and prepare for possible outcomes before they occur, transforming uncertainty into actionable expectation.
