DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A short, memorable phrase that is repeatedly used, recognized, and transmitted within a culture, group, audience, or communication system.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Facilitates the replication and spread of ideas, identities, messages, and associations through repetition and recall.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Advertising slogans  
- Political phrases  
- Movie quotes  
- Internet sayings  
- Brand taglines  
- Recurring media phrases  
- Cultural expressions

NOTES:  
  
Catchphrases often succeed because they are concise, memorable, repeatable, and easy to communicate. Their effectiveness is frequently enhanced through repetition, familiarity, emotional association, and cultural reinforcement.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The simplest ideas are often the easiest to transmit. A catchphrase compresses a larger concept, narrative, identity, or message into a form that can be rapidly remembered, repeated, and distributed across a population.
