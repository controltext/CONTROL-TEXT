SYSTEM [[08 SYMBOLIC POSSESSSION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A visual symbol designed to represent, identify, and communicate the identity of an organization, brand, institution, product, movement, or concept.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Condenses identity, recognition, reputation, and meaning into a simplified visual form that can be rapidly recognized and associated with specific ideas or entities.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Nike Swoosh  
- Apple Logo  
- McDonald's Golden Arches  
- Olympic Rings  
- Red Cross Symbol  
- Corporate Logos  
- Organizational Emblems

NOTES:  
  
Logos function as symbolic shortcuts that allow complex organizations, products, and identities to be recognized instantly. Over time, emotional associations, experiences, and narratives become attached to the logo itself.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The power of a logo rarely comes from its visual design alone. Its influence emerges from the meanings, memories, reputations, and expectations attached to the symbol through repeated exposure and experience.
