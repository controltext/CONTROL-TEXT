DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The organized record, interpretation, and understanding of past events, actions, developments, people, and periods across time.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Provides continuity between past and present by preserving, organizing, and communicating collective memory through temporal narratives and chronological frameworks.

- National histories  
- Personal histories  
- Cultural histories  
- Organizational histories  
- Historical records

NOTES:  
  
History organizes events into temporal frameworks that help individuals and groups understand origins, development, change, continuity, and causation. Historical interpretations may vary depending on perspective, evidence, emphasis, and narrative structure.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The past does not exist as an immediately accessible reality. History functions as a constructed framework through which events are selected, organized, interpreted, and communicated, allowing groups to establish continuity, identity, memory, and meaning across time.
