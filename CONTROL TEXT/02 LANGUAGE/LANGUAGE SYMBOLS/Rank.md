SYSTEMS [[15 THE NAME MACHINE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A classification that assigns an individual, group, object, concept, or entity a relative position within an ordered hierarchy based upon selected criteria, status, performance, authority, value, or importance.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Organizes categories into structured levels, allowing entities to be compared, ordered, evaluated, and distinguished according to a recognized system of hierarchy.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Military ranks  
- Organizational ranks  
- Academic rankings  
- Social status rankings  
- Competitive standings  
- Tier systems  
- Hierarchical classifications

NOTES:  
  
Ranks establish relative position within a system. The criteria used to determine rank may vary depending on the classification framework, institution, culture, or objective being applied.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Classification does not merely divide reality into categories; it often arranges those categories into hierarchies. Rank functions as a naming mechanism that assigns relative position, transforming differences into ordered structures through which authority, status, value, and importance can be communicated and understood.
