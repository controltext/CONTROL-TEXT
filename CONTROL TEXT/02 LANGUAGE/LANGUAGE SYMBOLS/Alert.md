SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A signal, message, warning, or notification intended to immediately attract attention by communicating urgency, importance, relevance, or potential significance.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Interrupts ongoing attention patterns and redirects focus toward a specific event, condition, threat, opportunity, or piece of information.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Emergency alerts  
- Breaking news notifications  
- Security warnings  
- Weather alerts  
- System error messages  
- Market alerts  
- Social media alerts

NOTES:  
  
Alerts derive their effectiveness from perceived importance and urgency. Frequent exposure to alerts may increase responsiveness, but excessive use can reduce sensitivity and attention over time.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Alerts compete for priority within consciousness. By signaling urgency or importance, they can temporarily override existing attention and redirect awareness toward selected information.
