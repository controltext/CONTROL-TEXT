SYSTEM [[13 TIME CONSTRUCTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A recurring date that commemorates, recognizes, or marks the passage of time since a significant event, occurrence, achievement, relationship, or milestone.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Preserves memory and significance by linking present time to past events through recurring temporal recognition.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Wedding anniversaries  
- Birth anniversaries  
- Company founding anniversaries  
- Historical commemorations  
- National anniversaries  
- Memorial observances  
- Organizational milestones

NOTES:  
  
Anniversaries create recurring moments of reflection, remembrance, celebration, or recognition by connecting present experience to past events. Their significance often depends upon cultural, personal, institutional, or historical meaning.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Time is not only measured but remembered. Anniversaries transform the passage of time into recurring acts of recognition, allowing individuals and groups to preserve identity, memory, relationships, and historical continuity across generations.
