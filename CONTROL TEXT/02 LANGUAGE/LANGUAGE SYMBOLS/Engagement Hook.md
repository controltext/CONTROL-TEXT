SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A feature, message, stimulus, question, reward, or interaction designed to capture attention and encourage continued participation, consumption, or engagement.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Converts initial attention into sustained interaction by creating curiosity, emotional investment, anticipation, relevance, or incentive.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Cliffhangers  
- Open-ended questions  
- "Like and Subscribe" prompts  
- Teaser content  
- Gamification mechanics  
- Reward systems  
- Interactive polls  
- Comment prompts

NOTES:  
  
Engagement hooks function by creating a reason to continue interacting. They often leverage curiosity, uncertainty, anticipation, emotional investment, rewards, social participation, or the desire for completion.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Capturing attention is only the first stage of engagement. Sustained participation often depends on creating unresolved questions, anticipated rewards, emotional investment, or perceived relevance that motivates continued interaction.
