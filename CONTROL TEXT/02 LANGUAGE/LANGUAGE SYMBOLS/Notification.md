SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A message, signal, alert, or prompt designed to attract attention and direct awareness toward a specific event, action, update, or piece of information.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Interrupts ongoing attention patterns in order to redirect focus toward a designated subject, behavior, platform, or communication.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Push notifications  
- Email alerts  
- Text message notifications  
- Social media notifications  
- Breaking news alerts  
- Software update prompts  
- Reminder messages

NOTES:  
  
Notifications compete for limited cognitive attention by creating awareness of new information, opportunities, threats, social interactions, or pending actions. Their effectiveness often depends on timing, relevance, frequency, and perceived importance.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Notifications function as attention-routing mechanisms. By interrupting existing focus and redirecting awareness, they influence what individuals notice, prioritize, remember, and respond to throughout the day.
