DEFINITION:  
  
A statement, instruction, directive, or expression intended to initiate, influence, organize, or direct behavior, action, attention, or response.

Transforms language into an action-oriented force by communicating expectations, intentions, instructions, or desired outcomes.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Verbal instructions  
- Military commands  
- Computer commands  
- Workplace directives  
- Parental instructions

CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Language does not merely describe reality—it can direct behavior. A command transforms words into action-oriented instructions that attempt to alter attention, decisions, movement, priorities, or outcomes through communication.
