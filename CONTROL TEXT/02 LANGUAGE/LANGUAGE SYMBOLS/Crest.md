SYSTEM [[08 SYMBOLIC POSSESSSION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A symbolic design, insignia, or heraldic mark used to represent identity, lineage, authority, membership, achievement, or affiliation.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Communicates status, heritage, legitimacy, and symbolic belonging through a recognizable visual emblem.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Family crests  
- Royal coats of arms  
- Military insignia  
- University seals  
- Organizational crests  
- Fraternal society emblems  
- Sports club crests

NOTES:  
  
Crests often function as symbolic representations of continuity, legitimacy, and collective identity. Their meaning is typically derived from historical associations, shared narratives, and social recognition rather than the visual design alone.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
A crest transforms abstract concepts such as heritage, authority, loyalty, and belonging into a visible symbol. The symbol itself becomes a container for identity and collective memory across generations.
