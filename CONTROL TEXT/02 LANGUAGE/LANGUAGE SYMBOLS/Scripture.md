NOTES:  
  
Scriptures often serve as enduring sources of guidance, meaning, authority, and interpretation. Their significance may derive from tradition, belief, historical importance, institutional recognition, or collective reverence.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Texts become powerful when they are repeatedly referenced, preserved, and treated as authoritative sources of meaning. Scripture functions as a mechanism through which teachings, values, narratives, and interpretations can persist across generations while maintaining continuity within a community or tradition.
