DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A short, memorable phrase designed to communicate, reinforce, and transmit a specific message, identity, value, narrative, or objective.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Condenses complex ideas into easily repeatable language that increases recall, recognition, emotional association, and narrative propagation.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- "Just Do It"  
- "Make America Great Again"  
- "Think Different"  
- "Black Lives Matter"  
- "The Customer Is Always Right"  
- "Knowledge Is Power"  
- Political campaign slogans

NOTES:  
  
Slogans simplify and package ideas into forms that are easy to remember, repeat, and distribute. Their effectiveness often depends on emotional resonance, repetition, and symbolic association rather than detailed explanation.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
A slogan can function as a linguistic container. Complex beliefs, identities, movements, and narratives can become compressed into a few words that are easily transmitted across individuals and groups.
