NOTES:  
  
Doctrines help preserve and distribute complex systems of thought by organizing ideas into coherent, repeatable forms. Their persistence often depends upon repetition, education, institutional support, and collective participation.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
While individual memes may spread rapidly, doctrines enable long-term replication. By organizing ideas into structured frameworks, doctrines increase the stability, consistency, and survivability of beliefs across time and populations.
