DEFINITION:

NOTES:  
  
Conversations differ from one-way communication because participants continuously exchange roles between sender and receiver. Meaning is often developed collaboratively through questions, responses, clarification, interpretation, feedback, and negotiation.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Meaning is not always transmitted in a single direction. Conversation functions as an interactive language process through which participants continuously construct, refine, challenge, and exchange understanding. Through dialogue, language becomes a living system capable of generating new interpretations, relationships, ideas, and shared realities.
