DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A projection, prediction, estimate, or anticipation of future events, conditions, developments, or outcomes based on available information, patterns, trends, or models.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Organizes expectations about the future by creating temporal frameworks that guide planning, preparation, decision-making, and behavior.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Weather forecasts  
- Economic forecasts  
- Election forecasts  
- Market projections  
- Population forecasts  
- Project estimates  
- Strategic outlooks

NOTES:  
  
Forecasts use information from the present and past to construct possible future scenarios. Their accuracy may vary depending on available data, assumptions, uncertainty, and changing conditions.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Time construction extends beyond recording the past and organizing the present. Forecasts create anticipated futures that influence behavior in the present, allowing individuals and groups to coordinate actions around expected outcomes before those outcomes occur.
