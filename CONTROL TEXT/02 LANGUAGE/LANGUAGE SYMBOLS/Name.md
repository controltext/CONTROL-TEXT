SYSTEM [[12 IDENTITY PROGRAMMING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A word, title, designation, or label used to identify, distinguish, and refer to an individual, group, object, role, concept, or entity.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Establishes identity by creating a symbolic reference through which recognition, association, categorization, and meaning can be assigned and communicated.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Personal names  
- Family names  
- Titles  
- Nicknames  
- Brand names  
- Organizational names  
- Group identities

NOTES:  
  
Names function as symbolic identifiers that allow individuals and groups to recognize, categorize, remember, and relate to entities. Over time, names may accumulate meanings, expectations, reputations, associations, and emotional significance beyond their original function.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
A name does more than identify. By assigning a symbolic label to a person, group, idea, or object, a name creates a point around which identity, meaning, reputation, and perception can accumulate and be communicated.
