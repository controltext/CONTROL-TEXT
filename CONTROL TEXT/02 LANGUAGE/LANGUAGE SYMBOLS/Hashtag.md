SYSTEM [[11 MEMETIC ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A keyword or phrase preceded by a symbol that groups, labels, and connects related content, conversations, ideas, or cultural phenomena within a communication network.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Facilitates the discovery, organization, replication, and spread of memes, narratives, trends, and collective participation around a shared concept.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- #BreakingNews  
- #ThrowbackThursday  
- #MeToo  
- #BlackLivesMatter  
- #Viral  
- Event hashtags  
- Campaign hashtags

NOTES:  
  
Hashtags function as memetic containers that allow ideas, discussions, movements, and trends to be grouped under a shared identifier. Their effectiveness often depends upon visibility, participation, repetition, and network adoption.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
A hashtag transforms a concept into a replicable label. By providing a common point of reference, hashtags enable distributed individuals to participate in, reinforce, and amplify shared narratives, trends, and cultural phenomena.
