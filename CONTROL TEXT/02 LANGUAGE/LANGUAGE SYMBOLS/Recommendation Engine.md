SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A system that selects, prioritizes, and presents content, information, products, people, or experiences based on predicted relevance, interest, behavior, or engagement.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Directs attention by determining what information is made visible, suggested, or promoted to an individual or audience.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- YouTube recommendations  
- TikTok For You Page  
- Netflix suggestions  
- Spotify playlists  
- Amazon product recommendations  
- Social media feeds  
- Search engine recommendations

NOTES:  
  
Recommendation engines influence attention by filtering large volumes of information and presenting selected options. Their outputs may be based on behavior, preferences, engagement patterns, popularity metrics, similarity models, or predictive analysis.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention is often directed not by the availability of information, but by the visibility of information. Recommendation systems function as attention gateways, influencing which ideas, narratives, products, and signals are encountered while others remain unseen.
