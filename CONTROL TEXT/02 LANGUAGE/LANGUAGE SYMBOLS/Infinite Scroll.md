SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A content delivery mechanism that continuously loads additional information as a user scrolls, eliminating fixed endpoints and creating an uninterrupted stream of content.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Extends attention duration by reducing friction, removing stopping cues, and providing a continuous flow of novel information.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Social media feeds  
- Short-form video platforms  
- News feeds  
- Content recommendation streams  
- Image sharing platforms  
- Video recommendation pages  
- Endless product catalogs

NOTES:  
  
Infinite scroll reduces the number of decisions required to access additional content. By continuously presenting new information, it can increase engagement, consumption time, and exposure to content streams.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention naturally disengages at boundaries. Infinite scroll minimizes boundaries by transforming information consumption into a continuous experience where the next piece of content is always immediately available.
