DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A physical structure, object, site, or landmark created to preserve, represent, commemorate, or symbolize a person, event, idea, achievement, identity, or collective memory.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms memory and meaning into durable symbolic form, allowing narratives, values, identities, and historical interpretations to persist across generations.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Statues  
- Memorials  
- Historical monuments  
- National landmarks  
- War memorials  
- Religious monuments  
- Cultural heritage sites

NOTES:  
  
Monuments function as symbolic anchors that preserve selected memories, identities, narratives, and interpretations of the past. Their significance often depends upon the meanings attributed to them by individuals and communities.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
A monument is not merely a structure. It is a symbolic claim about what deserves remembrance. Through permanence and visibility, monuments help shape collective memory, identity, and cultural continuity.
