SYSTEM [[09 ATTENTION EXTRACTION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
Content designed to attract attention and encourage engagement through curiosity, emotional stimulation, surprise, urgency, or incomplete information.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Captures attention by creating a perceived information gap that motivates individuals to seek additional information or interaction.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- "You Won't Believe What Happened Next"  
- Sensational headlines  
- Shocking thumbnails  
- Curiosity-driven titles  
- Outrage-inducing posts  
- Emotional teasers  
- Viral content hooks

NOTES:  
  
Clickbait often relies on curiosity, uncertainty, emotion, novelty, or urgency to encourage interaction. While the term is frequently used negatively, similar attention-capturing mechanisms are common throughout advertising, journalism, entertainment, and communication.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention is frequently captured through unanswered questions, emotional stimulation, and perceived information gaps. The promise of significance often attracts attention before significance itself is evaluated.
