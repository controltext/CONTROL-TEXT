SYSTEM [[08 SYMBOLIC POSSESSSION]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

  
DEFINITION:  
  
A character, figure, animal, person, or symbolic representation used to embody, personify, and communicate the identity, values, culture, or image of a group, organization, brand, institution, or movement.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms abstract identity into a recognizable personality that can generate attachment, familiarity, loyalty, and symbolic association.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Sports team mascots  
- Corporate mascots  
- National animal symbols  
- School mascots  
- Brand characters  
- Military unit mascots  
- Cultural symbolic figures

NOTES:  
  
Mascots make symbolic identities easier to recognize and emotionally engage with by presenting them in humanized, animalized, or character-based forms. The mascot often becomes a symbolic representative of the larger group or institution.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People frequently form stronger attachments to personalities than abstractions. A mascot allows a collective identity to be experienced as a character, making symbolic relationships more memorable, relatable, and emotionally engaging.
