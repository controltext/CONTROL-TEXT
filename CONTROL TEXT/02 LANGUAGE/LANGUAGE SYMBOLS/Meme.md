NOTES:  
  
Memes may exist as images, phrases, symbols, behaviors, stories, concepts, or cultural practices. Their persistence depends upon their ability to be remembered, repeated, modified, and transmitted across individuals and groups.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Ideas often compete for attention in the same way organisms compete for survival. Memes that are memorable, emotionally engaging, easily repeated, or socially reinforced tend to spread more effectively through populations.
