SYSTEM [[12 IDENTITY PROGRAMMING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A narrative account of an individual's life, experiences, actions, achievements, relationships, and development across time.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Organizes identity into a coherent story that communicates who a person is, where they came from, what they have done, and how they are understood.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Personal biographies  
- Professional biographies  
- Historical accounts  
- Autobiographies  
- Public profiles  
- Life stories  
- Personal histories

NOTES:  
  
Biographies transform collections of events, experiences, and characteristics into structured narratives. Different biographies of the same individual may emphasize different aspects of identity depending on perspective, purpose, and audience.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People frequently understand identity through stories. A biography organizes experiences into a narrative framework that creates continuity between past, present, and future, allowing identity to be communicated, remembered, and interpreted as a coherent whole.
