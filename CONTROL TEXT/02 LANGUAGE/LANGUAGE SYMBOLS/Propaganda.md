SYSTEM [[11 MEMETIC ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
Information, messaging, narratives, symbols, or communication designed to influence perceptions, beliefs, attitudes, interpretations, or behaviors within a population or audience.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Facilitates the transmission and reinforcement of ideas by shaping how information is presented, repeated, distributed, and interpreted.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Political campaigns  
- Wartime messaging  
- Public information campaigns  
- Ideological communications  
- Recruitment materials  
- Advocacy campaigns  
- Institutional messaging

NOTES:  
  
Propaganda may employ repetition, emotional appeal, symbolic language, selective framing, simplified narratives, slogans, and other communication techniques to increase the adoption and persistence of specific ideas.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Ideas often spread more effectively when they are deliberately packaged for transmission. Propaganda functions as a memetic distribution mechanism, increasing the visibility, repetition, and perceived legitimacy of selected messages within a communication environment.
