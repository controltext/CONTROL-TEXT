SYSTEMS [[15 THE NAME MACHINE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A classification that indicates the recognized position, standing, condition, level, or relative placement of an individual, group, object, or entity within a social, organizational, or conceptual framework.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Communicates relative position by assigning recognized levels of importance, authority, achievement, condition, membership, or recognition within a system.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Social status  
- Professional status  
- Legal status  
- Economic status  
- Membership status  
- Relationship status  
- Organizational status

NOTES:  
  
Status classifications allow systems to communicate distinctions between entities according to selected criteria. Status may be formal or informal, temporary or permanent, assigned or achieved, depending on the framework in which it exists.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Naming systems often do more than identify—they position. Status functions as a classification mechanism that assigns relative standing within a hierarchy, transforming differences into recognized levels of importance, authority, prestige, condition, or value.
