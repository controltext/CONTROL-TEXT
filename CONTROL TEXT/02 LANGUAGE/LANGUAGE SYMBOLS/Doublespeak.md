SYSTEM [[07 SEMANTIC WARFARE]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
Language that obscures, disguises, reverses, distorts, or conceals meaning in order to influence perception, reduce clarity, or alter interpretation.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Separates words from direct reality by using terminology that masks, reframes, or redirects attention away from the underlying subject.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- "Enhanced Interrogation" instead of torture  
- "Collateral Damage" instead of civilian deaths  
- "Negative Growth" instead of decline  
- "Workforce Optimization" instead of layoffs  
- "Peacekeeping Operation" instead of military intervention  
- "Correctional Facility" instead of prison

NOTES:  
  
Doublespeak functions by creating distance between language and direct description. The resulting terminology may reduce emotional impact, alter interpretation, obscure responsibility, or redirect attention toward a preferred narrative.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
When language becomes detached from direct description, interpretation increasingly depends on definitions, context, and authority. The ability to redefine meaning can influence perception without changing underlying reality.
