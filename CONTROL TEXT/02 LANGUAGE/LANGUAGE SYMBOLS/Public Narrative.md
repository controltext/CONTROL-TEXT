SYSTEM [[10 CONSENSUS REALITY]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A widely shared story, interpretation, explanation, or framework through which a group, culture, institution, or society understands events, issues, identities, or reality.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Provides a common interpretive framework that influences how individuals perceive, discuss, and understand collective experiences and social reality.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- National narratives  
- Historical interpretations  
- Economic narratives  
- Cultural narratives  
- Media narratives  
- Social movement narratives  
- Public explanations of major events

NOTES:  
  
Public narratives often emerge through the interaction of media, institutions, culture, education, tradition, and collective discussion. Multiple competing public narratives may exist simultaneously within the same society.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People frequently understand complex realities through shared stories rather than isolated facts. Public narratives organize events into coherent explanations that help groups establish meaning, identity, causation, and collective understanding.
