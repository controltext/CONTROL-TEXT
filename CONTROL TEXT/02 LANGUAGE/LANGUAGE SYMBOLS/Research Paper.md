NOTES:  
  
Research papers serve as formal records of inquiry and analysis. They often contribute to larger bodies of knowledge through documentation, review, citation, critique, replication, and ongoing scholarly discussion.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Knowledge becomes durable when it is documented. Research papers transform observations and investigations into transmissible textual artifacts that can be preserved, referenced, challenged, expanded, and integrated into broader systems of understanding across time.
