SYSTEM [[10 CONSENSUS REALITY]]
DOMAIN [[THE LANGUAGE]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A belief, assumption, interpretation, or understanding that is widely accepted, shared, or repeated within a group, culture, community, or society.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Influences perception and behavior by providing commonly accepted explanations, assumptions, values, and understandings that individuals use to interpret reality.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Cultural assumptions  
- Common wisdom  
- Widely accepted narratives  
- Social expectations  
- Shared interpretations  
- Public attitudes

NOTES:  
  
Popular beliefs may emerge through experience, tradition, education, repetition, social interaction, media exposure, or collective reinforcement. Their popularity does not necessarily determine their accuracy, but it often influences their perceived legitimacy.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People frequently inherit beliefs from the environments in which they participate. Repeated exposure and widespread acceptance can transform ideas into assumptions that become part of the perceived structure of reality.
