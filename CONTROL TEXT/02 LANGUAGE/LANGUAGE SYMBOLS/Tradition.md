NOTES:  
  
Traditions can persist across long periods of time through repetition, imitation, participation, and social reinforcement. Their continuation often depends upon collective acceptance rather than formal enforcement.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Traditions create stability within collective reality by carrying beliefs, behaviors, values, and interpretations forward through time. Repetition across generations can transform practices into assumptions, and assumptions into perceived realities.
