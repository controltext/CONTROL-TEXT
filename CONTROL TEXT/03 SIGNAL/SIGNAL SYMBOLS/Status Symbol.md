SYSTEMS [[21 REPUTATION SYSTEMS]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]


DEFINITION:  
  
An object, marker, possession, credential, association, achievement, title, attribute, or visible signal that communicates elevated social status, prestige, success, influence, wealth, recognition, or distinction within a social system.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Acts as a shorthand signal that allows observers to infer status, reputation, prestige, credibility, success, belonging, or social standing without requiring detailed investigation.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Luxury goods  
- Professional titles  
- Elite credentials  
- Awards and trophies  
- High-status memberships  
- Verification badges  
- Exclusive access privileges

NOTES:  
  
Status symbols derive their power from collective recognition. The same object or marker may function as a status symbol in one social group while carrying little significance in another. Their meaning depends upon shared social interpretation.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Complex social hierarchies are often communicated through simple signals. A status symbol functions as a reputation-compression mechanism that converts prestige, success, influence, authority, or social standing into visible markers. Through status symbols, reputation becomes immediately recognizable, allowing social position to be communicated rapidly within a community or system.
