SYSTEM [[23 EMOTIONAL ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
An emotional state characterized by satisfaction, dignity, self-respect, accomplishment, confidence, esteem, fulfillment, or affirmation arising from perceived achievement, competence, virtue, identity, success, recognition, or alignment with valued standards.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Reinforces behaviors, identities, achievements, values, and accomplishments by associating them with positive emotional significance and self-evaluation.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Pride in achievement  
- Pride in craftsmanship  
- Pride in family  
- Pride in identity  
- Pride in service  
- Pride in accomplishment  
- Pride in mastery

NOTES:  
  
Pride may be healthy or unhealthy depending on context, proportion, and expression. It may arise from genuine accomplishment, personal growth, social recognition, group identity, status, competence, or perceived superiority.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Humans often repeat behaviors that affirm identity and self-worth. Pride functions as a reinforcement mechanism that links achievements, values, competencies, and identities to positive emotional significance. Through pride, accomplishments become meaningful, identities become emotionally invested, and behaviors become self-reinforcing through recognition and internal satisfaction.
