DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
An initial idea, symbol, concept, image, theme, possibility, message, story element, or future-oriented suggestion introduced into an information environment that may later develop into a larger narrative structure.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Plants future interpretive frameworks by introducing concepts before they become widespread, familiar, expected, normalized, or culturally significant.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Early science fiction concepts  
- Emerging future technologies  
- Experimental social ideas  
- Prototype narratives  
- Cultural thought experiments  
- Symbolic future imagery  
- New societal possibilities

NOTES:  
  
Narrative seeds are often small, ambiguous, symbolic, speculative, or seemingly insignificant when first introduced. Their significance may emerge later as they connect with broader cultural developments, events, technologies, or narratives.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Large narratives often begin as small ideas. A narrative seed functions as a future-orientation mechanism that introduces conceptual possibilities into collective awareness before they become dominant stories, expectations, or realities. Through repetition, development, adaptation, and amplification, narrative seeds can grow into scenarios, visions, forecasts, myths, movements, and cultural frameworks that shape how future events are interpreted.
