SYSTEM [[24 SURVEILLANCE SOCIETY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A device, system, sensor, or instrument used to capture, record, transmit, store, or observe visual information from an environment, event, object, individual, or activity.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms physical events and visual experiences into observable, recordable, reviewable, and analyzable information.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Security cameras  
- CCTV systems  
- Traffic cameras  
- Body cameras  
- Doorbell cameras  
- Smartphone cameras  
- Webcams

NOTES:  
  
Cameras may operate in real time or record information for later review. Their impact depends upon placement, visibility, storage, analysis capabilities, access controls, and integration with other systems.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Observation becomes scalable when it is automated and recorded. A camera functions as a visual data-acquisition mechanism that transforms transient events into persistent records. Through cameras, environments become observable across time and distance, enabling monitoring, documentation, analysis, accountability, identification, and behavioral influence.
