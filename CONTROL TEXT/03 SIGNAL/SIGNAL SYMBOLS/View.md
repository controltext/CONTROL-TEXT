SYSTEM [[25 ATTENTION ECONOMY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
An instance of visual attention directed toward content, information, media, an object, an event, a message, or a source of communication.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms attention into a measurable unit by recording or recognizing that content has been seen, observed, consumed, or exposed to an audience.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Video views  
- Page views  
- Advertisement views  
- Livestream views  
- Social media views  
- News article views  
- Content impressions

NOTES:  
  
A view measures exposure, not necessarily understanding, agreement, trust, engagement, or retention. Different systems may count views according to different criteria and thresholds.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention becomes manageable when it becomes measurable. A view functions as an attention-unit mechanism that converts awareness into quantifiable data. Through views, visibility becomes measurable, attention becomes comparable, popularity becomes countable, and informational competition becomes possible within large-scale media environments.
