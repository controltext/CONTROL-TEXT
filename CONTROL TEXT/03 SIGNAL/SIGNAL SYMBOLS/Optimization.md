SYSTEM [[20 ALGORITHMIC GOVERNANCE]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The process of modifying, refining, adjusting, or improving a system, model, process, ranking, recommendation, or decision-making mechanism in order to maximize, minimize, or achieve selected objectives according to defined criteria.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Guides system behavior by directing resources, attention, decisions, and outputs toward preferred outcomes through continuous measurement and adjustment.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Search engine optimization  
- Recommendation optimization  
- Advertising optimization  
- Engagement optimization  
- Route optimization  
- Performance optimization  
- Resource allocation optimization

NOTES:  
  
Optimization depends upon selected goals and metrics. Different objectives may produce different outcomes even when using the same underlying system. What is optimized often determines how the system behaves.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Systems do not merely operate—they adapt. Optimization functions as a goal-seeking mechanism that continuously modifies rankings, recommendations, priorities, and decisions in response to measured outcomes. By defining what success means, optimization influences what behaviors, information, actions, and outcomes become increasingly favored over time.
