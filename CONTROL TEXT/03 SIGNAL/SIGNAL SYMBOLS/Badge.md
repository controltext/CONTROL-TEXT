SYSTEMS [[21 REPUTATION SYSTEMS]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A symbol, marker, credential, indicator, certification, emblem, or visible designation used to communicate achievement, status, trustworthiness, membership, qualification, verification, or recognition.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Provides a quick visual signal that communicates reputation, accomplishment, authority, credibility, identity, or standing within a system.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Verification badges  
- Achievement badges  
- Certification badges  
- Membership badges  
- Loyalty program badges  
- Skill badges  
- Professional credentials

NOTES:  
  
Badges may be earned, assigned, granted, purchased, verified, or automatically generated depending upon the governing system. Their influence often depends upon recognition, legitimacy, scarcity, trust, and the reputation of the issuing authority.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Complex reputations are often condensed into simple symbols. A badge functions as a reputation-compression mechanism that transforms achievements, credentials, trust signals, status markers, or qualifications into immediately recognizable indicators, allowing reputation to be communicated rapidly within large social systems.
