SYSTEM [[24 SURVEILLANCE SOCIETY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A device, system, mechanism, process, or tool used to monitor, record, follow, measure, observe, or document the movement, activity, behavior, status, location, condition, or progression of an entity across time.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms isolated observations into continuous histories by linking events, movements, actions, locations, or behaviors into trackable sequences.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- GPS trackers  
- Activity trackers  
- Fitness trackers  
- Vehicle trackers  
- Asset tracking systems  
- Shipment tracking systems  
- Behavioral tracking systems

NOTES:  
  
Trackers may monitor physical movement, digital activity, communications, transactions, behaviors, biological conditions, environmental changes, or other measurable phenomena. Their significance often derives from continuity over time rather than isolated observations.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Single observations reveal moments. Tracking reveals patterns. A tracker functions as a continuity-generation mechanism that connects separate observations into coherent histories. Through tracking, movement becomes a route, behavior becomes a pattern, activity becomes a profile, and isolated events become timelines that can be analyzed, predicted, classified, and acted upon.
