SYSTEM [[19 MANUFACTURED CONSENT]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A formal statement, declaration, conclusion, recommendation, or position presented as representing the collective agreement, judgment, understanding, or viewpoint of a group, institution, organization, panel, committee, or body of participants.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms multiple individual opinions, interpretations, or perspectives into a unified public position that can be communicated as a collective conclusion.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Scientific consensus statements  
- Professional association statements  
- Expert panel conclusions  
- Organizational position statements  
- Policy recommendations  
- Committee findings  
- Institutional declarations

NOTES:  
  
Consensus statements may emerge through discussion, voting, negotiation, evidence review, deliberation, committee processes, or institutional procedures. Their influence often derives from the perceived credibility, expertise, authority, or legitimacy of the group issuing the statement.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Groups often possess greater persuasive influence than isolated individuals. A consensus statement functions as a legitimacy-generation mechanism that transforms multiple viewpoints into a single authoritative position, increasing perceived certainty, agreement, stability, and social acceptance through the communication of collective endorsement.
