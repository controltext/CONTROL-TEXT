SYSTEM [[25 ATTENTION ECONOMY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
An instance in which content, information, media, a message, an advertisement, an image, or a communication becomes visible or available to an audience member, creating the opportunity for attention.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Measures exposure potential by recording when content appears within an observer's field of awareness, regardless of whether interaction occurs.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Advertisement impressions  
- Social media impressions  
- Search result impressions  
- Feed impressions  
- Website impressions  
- Banner impressions  
- Video thumbnail impressions

NOTES:  
  
An impression indicates that content was displayed or made available for viewing. It does not necessarily mean the content was consciously noticed, understood, remembered, or engaged with.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention begins with opportunity. An impression functions as an exposure-generation mechanism that places signals into the field of potential awareness. Through impressions, content competes for limited attention resources, creating the conditions under which views, clicks, engagement, influence, and value may emerge.
