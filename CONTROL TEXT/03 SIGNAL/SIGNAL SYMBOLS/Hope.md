SYSTEM [[23 EMOTIONAL ENGINEERING]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
An emotional state characterized by the expectation, anticipation, desire, belief, or possibility that favorable outcomes, improvements, opportunities, solutions, achievements, or positive transformations may occur.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Directs attention toward desired futures, sustains motivation during uncertainty, supports perseverance, encourages action, and organizes behavior around anticipated positive outcomes.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Hope for recovery  
- Hope for success  
- Hope for peace  
- Hope for opportunity  
- Hope for transformation  
- Hope for prosperity  
- Hope for a better future

NOTES:  
  
Hope may arise from evidence, belief, imagination, trust, faith, resilience, goals, relationships, or future-oriented narratives. Like fear, hope may be proportional or disproportionate to objective conditions.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Humans are often pulled forward by imagined possibilities. Hope functions as an attention-orientation mechanism that directs awareness toward desired futures rather than perceived threats. By linking present action to anticipated positive outcomes, hope influences motivation, endurance, interpretation, identity, cooperation, and long-term behavior.
