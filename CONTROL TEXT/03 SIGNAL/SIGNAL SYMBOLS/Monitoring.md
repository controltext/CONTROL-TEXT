SYSTEM [[24 SURVEILLANCE SOCIETY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The ongoing observation, measurement, recording, tracking, supervision, evaluation, or oversight of individuals, groups, systems, environments, activities, communications, behaviors, or conditions across time.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms isolated observations into continuous awareness by maintaining persistent attention on selected subjects, activities, locations, conditions, or events.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Security monitoring  
- Network monitoring  
- Employee monitoring  
- Environmental monitoring  
- Behavioral monitoring  
- Health monitoring  
- Communications monitoring

NOTES:  
  
Monitoring may be manual, automated, intermittent, continuous, visible, or invisible. Its effectiveness often depends upon sensor coverage, data quality, analytical systems, response mechanisms, and persistence over time.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Observation captures moments. Monitoring captures continuity. Monitoring functions as a persistence mechanism that maintains attention across time, transforming isolated events into ongoing awareness. Through monitoring, behaviors become patterns, activities become histories, anomalies become detectable, and environments become continuously observable.
