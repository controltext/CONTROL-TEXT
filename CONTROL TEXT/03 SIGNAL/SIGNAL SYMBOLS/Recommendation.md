SYSTEM [[20 ALGORITHMIC GOVERNANCE]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A suggestion, ranking, selection, or presentation of options intended to guide attention, decisions, preferences, actions, or behavior toward particular choices.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Influences decision-making by filtering, prioritizing, and presenting selected options from a larger set of possibilities.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Product recommendations  
- Video recommendations  
- Music suggestions  
- News feeds  
- Search result rankings  
- Friend recommendations  
- Content suggestions

NOTES:  
  
Recommendations may be generated by humans, institutions, algorithms, social networks, or hybrid systems. Their influence often depends upon visibility, trust, convenience, personalization, relevance, and perceived authority.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Most choices occur within environments where not every option is equally visible. A recommendation functions as a selection mechanism that narrows possibility space by elevating certain options above others, thereby influencing attention, discovery, preference formation, and decision-making without requiring direct coercion.
