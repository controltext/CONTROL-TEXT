SYSTEMS [[22 PREDICTIVE PROGRAMMING]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A structured conception, image, narrative, expectation, aspiration, projection, or model of a possible future condition, reality, society, outcome, or state of existence.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Organizes perception and decision-making by providing a future-oriented reference point toward which attention, planning, behavior, interpretation, resources, and collective action may be directed.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Strategic visions  
- Technological futures  
- Civilizational visions  
- Utopian futures  
- Long-term plans  
- Cultural future narratives  
- Organizational future roadmaps

NOTES:  
  
Future visions may be optimistic, pessimistic, neutral, symbolic, strategic, ideological, practical, or aspirational. They need not be accurate predictions; their significance often lies in their ability to coordinate attention and action in the present.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Humans frequently act toward imagined futures. A future vision functions as an expectation-coordination mechanism that transforms abstract possibilities into coherent destinations. By providing a picture of what might become, future visions influence planning, investment, identity formation, innovation, interpretation, motivation, and collective behavior long before the envisioned future exists.
