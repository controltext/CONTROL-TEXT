DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A statement, assessment, interpretation, recommendation, judgment, or conclusion offered by an individual or group recognized as possessing specialized knowledge, training, experience, credentials, or expertise within a particular field.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Influences perception and decision-making by associating information, interpretations, recommendations, or conclusions with a recognized source of expertise and authority.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Scientific opinions  
- Medical recommendations  
- Legal analyses  
- Technical assessments  
- Economic forecasts  
- Professional evaluations  
- Specialist commentary

NOTES:  
  
Expert opinions derive influence from perceived competence, experience, training, credentials, specialization, or demonstrated knowledge. Different experts may reach different conclusions depending on evidence, assumptions, methodologies, interpretations, and professional frameworks.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People often rely upon specialized knowledge they do not personally possess. Expert opinion functions as a trust-allocation mechanism through which individuals and groups defer portions of their decision-making and interpretation processes to recognized authorities, thereby shaping perception, legitimacy, belief, and collective understanding.
