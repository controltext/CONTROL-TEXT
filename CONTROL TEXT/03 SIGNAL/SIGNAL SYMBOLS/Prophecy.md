SYSTEMS [[22 PREDICTIVE PROGRAMMING]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A statement, vision, revelation, prediction, pronouncement, or narrative that describes, anticipates, or claims knowledge of future events, developments, outcomes, conditions, or destinies.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Projects future possibilities into the present, shaping expectations, interpretations, beliefs, preparations, and collective imagination regarding what may occur.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Religious prophecies  
- Oracular predictions  
- Apocalyptic visions  
- Messianic forecasts  
- Cultural prophecies  
- Historical predictions  
- Mythological future narratives

NOTES:  
  
Prophecies may be interpreted literally, symbolically, metaphorically, spiritually, politically, culturally, or psychologically. Their influence often depends upon belief, authority, tradition, interpretation, and collective acceptance.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Future narratives gain power when they are believed. A prophecy functions as an expectation-generating mechanism that transforms imagined futures into meaningful possibilities. By framing future events as anticipated, destined, revealed, or inevitable, prophecies influence present behavior, interpretation, planning, hope, fear, and collective orientation toward the future.
