SYSTEM [[25 ATTENTION ECONOMY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The process or event in which attention, interest, engagement, or audience activity results in a specific intended action, outcome, commitment, transaction, decision, or behavioral change.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms audience attention into measurable results by linking signal exposure and engagement to concrete actions.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Product purchases  
- Newsletter signups  
- Channel subscriptions  
- Membership enrollments  
- Donations  
- Downloads  
- Lead generation  
- Account creation

NOTES:  
  
A conversion is defined by the objective of the system measuring it. Different systems may define conversions differently depending on desired outcomes, goals, incentives, or business models.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention alone does not guarantee results. Conversion functions as an outcome-generation mechanism that transforms awareness into action. Through conversion, signals move beyond visibility and engagement into measurable behavioral effects, allowing attention to be linked directly to decisions, commitments, transactions, and value creation.
