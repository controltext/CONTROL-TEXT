SYSTEM [[19 MANUFACTURED CONSENT]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A public statement, signal, recommendation, approval, support, or validation through which an individual, group, institution, organization, or authority expresses favorable recognition toward a person, idea, product, policy, movement, or position.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Influences perception by transferring credibility, legitimacy, trust, authority, reputation, or social acceptance from the endorser to the endorsed target.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Political endorsements  
- Celebrity endorsements  
- Expert recommendations  
- Product testimonials  
- Organizational support statements  
- Public approvals  
- Institutional backing

NOTES:  
  
Endorsements may derive influence from the perceived expertise, popularity, authority, credibility, status, reputation, or trustworthiness of the endorser. Their effectiveness often depends upon audience perception of the source.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People frequently evaluate ideas through social signals rather than direct investigation alone. Endorsements function as trust-transfer mechanisms that communicate approval and legitimacy, influencing perception by associating a target with a recognized source of credibility, authority, reputation, or social acceptance.
