DOMAIN [[THE SIMULATION]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The formation, exploration, discussion, or communication of possible explanations, outcomes, events, conditions, developments, or futures based upon incomplete information, assumptions, intuition, inference, imagination, or limited evidence.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Expands the space of possible futures by generating hypotheses, possibilities, interpretations, and imagined outcomes beyond currently established knowledge.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Market speculation  
- Political speculation  
- Technological speculation  
- Future forecasting discussions  
- Strategic speculation  
- Cultural speculation  
- Science fiction concepts

NOTES:  
  
Speculation is not prediction. It explores what might be true or what might occur without claiming certainty. Speculation may be informed, uninformed, creative, analytical, evidence-based, symbolic, or imaginative.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The future often begins as possibility before becoming probability. Speculation functions as a possibility-generation mechanism that expands the range of imaginable outcomes beyond what is currently known. By creating hypothetical futures, speculation influences expectations, innovation, planning, interpretation, curiosity, fear, hope, and collective imagination regarding what could emerge from the present.
