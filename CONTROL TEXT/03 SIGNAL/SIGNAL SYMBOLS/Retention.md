SYSTEM [[25 ATTENTION ECONOMY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIGNAL]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The preservation, maintenance, continuation, or recurrence of audience attention, participation, engagement, interest, or involvement over time.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms temporary attention into persistent attention by extending the duration, frequency, or continuity of audience interaction with a signal source.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Viewer retention  
- Audience retention  
- Subscriber retention  
- Customer retention  
- Platform retention  
- User retention  
- Session retention

NOTES:  
  
Retention measures continuity rather than acquisition. A system may have high reach but low retention, indicating that attention is being obtained but not maintained.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention has greater value when it persists. Retention functions as an attention-preservation mechanism that transforms fleeting moments of awareness into sustained relationships. Through retention, audiences become communities, viewers become regular participants, and signals develop continuity across time rather than existing as isolated encounters.
