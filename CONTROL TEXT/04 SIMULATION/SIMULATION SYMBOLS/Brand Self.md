SYSTEMS [[27 MANUFACTURED IDENTITY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIMULATION]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A consciously constructed identity in which an individual organizes, presents, manages, markets, and communicates themselves as a recognizable brand composed of symbols, narratives, values, aesthetics, associations, reputations, and perceived qualities.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms personal identity into a symbolic asset by creating consistency, recognition, differentiation, memorability, and influence within social, cultural, economic, and informational systems.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Personal brands  
- Creator brands  
- Influencer brands  
- Professional brands  
- Entrepreneur brands  
- Celebrity brands  
- Online identity brands

NOTES:  
  
A brand self may emerge intentionally or unintentionally. In modern networked environments, individuals are frequently encouraged to curate, manage, optimize, and communicate their identities as recognizable brands.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Identity increasingly operates within environments designed around visibility and recognition. A brand self functions as an identity-commercialization mechanism that transforms personal characteristics into symbolic assets. Through brand selves, personalities become products, reputations become resources, narratives become positioning strategies, and identity becomes something that can be cultivated, differentiated, amplified, and managed over time.
