DEFINITION:

NOTES:  
  
A collective mind does not require perfect agreement among participants. It emerges from ongoing interaction, shared symbols, common narratives, recurring behaviors, and mutual influence.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Human beings rarely think in isolation. A collective mind functions as an intelligence-emergence mechanism that transforms many individual perspectives into a larger pattern of cognition. Through collective minds, beliefs become cultures, symbols become realities, narratives become identities, and communities acquire properties greater than any individual participant. The Egregore Network emerges when collective minds begin to influence the individuals that created them.
