Movements may be formal or informal, centralized or decentralized, temporary or enduring. Their defining characteristic is the coordination of collective energy toward a shared direction.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Collective minds often seek expression through action. A movement functions as a momentum-generation mechanism that transforms shared beliefs into organized participation. Through movements, narratives become missions, identities become commitments, and collective intentions become forces capable of reshaping institutions, cultures, nations, and realities. Within the Egregore Network, movements represent egregores in motion.
