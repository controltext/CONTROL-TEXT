CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Understanding requires organization. A framework functions as a reality-organization mechanism that arranges concepts into usable structures. Through frameworks, information gains context, assumptions gain relationships, beliefs gain coherence, and complex realities become navigable. Frameworks determine not only what is understood, but how understanding itself is constructed.
