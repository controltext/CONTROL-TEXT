CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Some collective entities acquire lives of their own. A corporation functions as an organizational-egregore mechanism that transforms coordinated individuals into a persistent actor. Through corporations, identities become brands, narratives become missions, cultures become operating systems, and institutions become engines of action. Within the Egregore Network, corporations represent modern collective beings capable of influencing markets, cultures, technologies, governments, and the perceptions of millions of people.
