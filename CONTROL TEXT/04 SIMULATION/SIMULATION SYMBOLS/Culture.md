Collective minds require environments in which beliefs can persist and reproduce. Culture functions as a reality-maintenance mechanism that transforms shared meanings into everyday experience. Through culture, myths become traditions, symbols become habits, identities become expectations, and institutions become normalized. The Egregore Network emerges when collective patterns become self-reinforcing systems capable of shaping the individuals born into them.
