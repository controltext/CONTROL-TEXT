NOTES:  
  
A hive mind does not necessarily eliminate individuality. It describes systems where collective coordination emerges through communication, feedback, shared signals, common goals, or recurring behavioral patterns.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Intelligence may emerge from networks as well as individuals. A hive mind functions as a coordination-emergence mechanism that transforms many independent actors into a larger adaptive system. Through hive minds, information propagates rapidly, behaviors synchronize, and collective responses emerge without centralized control. The Egregore Network emerges when coordinated intelligence begins exhibiting patterns that transcend the intentions of any single participant.
