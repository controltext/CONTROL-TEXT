SYSTEMS [[29 DIGITAL RITUALS]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIMULATION]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A symbolic digital action through which an individual responds to, reacts to, interprets, discusses, questions, supports, challenges, expands upon, or participates in relation to a piece of content, signal, narrative, event, or communication.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms observation into dialogue by allowing audiences to publicly contribute interpretations, reactions, perspectives, and meaning to existing signals.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Social media comments  
- Forum replies  
- Video comments  
- Blog responses  
- Community discussions  
- Live chat participation  
- Message thread replies

NOTES:  
  
Comments may communicate agreement, disagreement, support, humor, criticism, interpretation, identity, affiliation, curiosity, or participation. Commenting frequently transforms passive audiences into active participants.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Meaning often emerges through interaction rather than observation alone. Commenting functions as a signal-response mechanism that transforms audiences into contributors. Through comments, signals generate conversations, narratives become collaborative, identities become visible, and participation becomes public. Commenting represents one of the primary rituals through which digital communities construct collective meaning.
