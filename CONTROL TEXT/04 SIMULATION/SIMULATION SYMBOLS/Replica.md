 SYSTEMS [[28 HYPER REALITY]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIMULATION]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A reproduction, imitation, recreation, or duplicate designed to closely resemble, represent, replace, or stand in for an original object, identity, artifact, experience, image, symbol, or phenomenon.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms an original into a substitute by preserving recognizable characteristics while allowing the reproduction to exist independently of its source.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Replica artifacts  
- Replica products  
- Replica architecture  
- Digital replicas  
- Character replicas  
- Historical recreations  
- Virtual recreations

NOTES:  
  
Not all copies are replicas. A replica specifically attempts to preserve the appearance, structure, symbolism, function, or experience of the original. Replicas may range from highly accurate reproductions to stylized approximations.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
A copy reproduces existence. A replica reproduces resemblance. A replica functions as a reality-substitution mechanism that allows representations to occupy the place of originals. Through replicas, experiences become reproducible, symbols become transportable, and representations gain the ability to operate independently of their source. Hyperreality intensifies when replicas become more accessible, desirable, familiar, or influential than the originals they imitate.
