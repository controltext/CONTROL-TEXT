SYSTEMS [[29 DIGITAL RITUALS]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIMULATION]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A symbolic digital action through which an individual publishes, uploads, broadcasts, shares, contributes, or releases content into a digital environment for others to perceive, interpret, engage with, or respond to.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms private expression into public signal by allowing individuals to insert information, narratives, identities, opinions, experiences, or symbols into shared digital spaces.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Social media posts  
- Forum posts  
- Status updates  
- Photo uploads  
- Video uploads  
- Blog posts  
- Community contributions

NOTES:  
  
Posting may serve purposes such as communication, self-expression, identity construction, community participation, documentation, persuasion, entertainment, education, or social signaling.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Digital participation often begins with expression. A posting functions as a signal-generation mechanism that transforms thoughts, experiences, identities, and narratives into publicly accessible artifacts. Through posting, individuals become broadcasters, identities become visible, and participation becomes observable. Posting is one of the primary rituals through which digital realities are continuously created and maintained.
