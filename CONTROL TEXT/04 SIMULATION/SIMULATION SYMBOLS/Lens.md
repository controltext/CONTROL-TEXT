NOTES:  
  
Different lenses may produce radically different interpretations of identical events. A lens may clarify certain aspects of reality while obscuring others. Multiple lenses may coexist simultaneously.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Reality is rarely experienced without mediation. A lens functions as a perception-shaping mechanism that determines how observations are brought into focus and interpreted. Through lenses, observers emphasize certain patterns, meanings, relationships, and explanations while minimizing others. Reality tunnels are often composed of multiple overlapping lenses operating simultaneously.
