Assumptions may be conscious or unconscious, accurate or inaccurate, useful or limiting. Most assumptions operate beneath awareness and often remain invisible until challenged by conflicting information or experience.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
No model of reality begins from absolute certainty. Assumptions function as reality-construction primitives that provide the initial conditions from which interpretations, beliefs, and worldviews are built. Through assumptions, observers fill gaps in knowledge, generate expectations, create explanations, and construct coherent simulations of reality. Every reality tunnel rests upon assumptions that are often mistaken for facts.
