SYSTEMS [[31 REALITY MARKETS]]
DOMAIN [[THE SIMULATION]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A source, reservoir, channel, audience, platform, identity, community, signal stream, or informational position that possesses value because it reliably captures, concentrates, directs, retains, or redistributes human attention.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms attention into value by converting awareness, visibility, engagement, and audience focus into a tradeable resource capable of influencing future behavior.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Social media audiences  
- YouTube channels  
- News platforms  
- Influencer audiences  
- Subscriber bases  
- Brand visibility  
- Media properties  
- High-traffic websites

NOTES:  
  
Attention assets derive value not from physical ownership alone but from their capacity to attract and direct human awareness. Their worth often depends upon audience size, audience quality, engagement, trust, retention, and influence.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Attention functions as one of the most valuable resources in informational environments. An attention asset functions as an awareness-valuation mechanism that transforms visibility into market value. Through attention assets, audiences become resources, engagement becomes capital, and awareness becomes an exchangeable force. Reality markets emerge when the ability to direct attention becomes more valuable than ownership of physical assets themselves.
