NOTES:  
  
Different perspectives may observe the same reality while producing different interpretations. Perspective does not necessarily determine truth or falsehood; it determines the angle from which information is perceived.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Reality appears differently depending upon where one is standing. Perspective functions as an observation-orientation mechanism that determines how information enters awareness and acquires meaning. Through perspective, identical events may generate different conclusions, emotions, narratives, and interpretations. A change in perspective can alter an entire reality tunnel without changing the underlying event itself.
