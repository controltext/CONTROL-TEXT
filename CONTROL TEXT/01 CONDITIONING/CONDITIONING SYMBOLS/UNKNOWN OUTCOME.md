SYSTEM [[02 Existential Deconstruction]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The inability to know with certainty the future consequences, results, or destinations of actions, events, and processes.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Introduces unpredictability into decision-making by preventing complete certainty about what will happen next.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Future life events  
- Economic changes  
- Relationship outcomes  
- Career paths  
- Health conditions  
- Technological developments  
- Global events

NOTES:  
  
Unknown outcome exists because reality contains variables, interactions, and conditions that cannot be fully anticipated or controlled. Human beings often attempt to reduce uncertainty through prediction, planning, and narrative construction.
