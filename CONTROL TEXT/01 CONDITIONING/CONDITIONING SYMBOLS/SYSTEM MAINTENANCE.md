DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The ongoing preservation, reinforcement, and operation of existing structures, institutions, rules, and power relationships.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Maintains stability and continuity by protecting systems from disruption, collapse, replacement, or significant change.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Bureaucratic processes  
- Institutional traditions  
- Regulatory enforcement  
- Corporate governance  
- Administrative oversight  
- Compliance programs  
- Organizational procedures

System maintenance often occurs through routine operations rather than overt control. Many systems persist because countless individuals participate in their continued operation, often without consciously considering the larger structure.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Power is rarely sustained through force alone. Long-lasting systems often endure because their maintenance becomes normalized, automated, or culturally expected.
