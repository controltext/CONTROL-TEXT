SYSTEM [[04 Power Corruption]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The monitoring, tracking, observation, and collection of information used to maintain authority, enforce compliance, and preserve control within a system.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Provides visibility into behavior, participation, performance, and dissent, allowing power structures to assess, influence, and respond to individuals or groups.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Workplace monitoring  
- Government intelligence gathering  
- Performance reviews  
- Security cameras  
- Employee tracking  
- Compliance audits  
- Digital activity monitoring

NOTES:  
  
Power surveillance enables systems to gather information about participants, identify patterns, detect deviations, and evaluate compliance. Surveillance may be visible, hidden, automated, or decentralized.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The ability to observe often increases the ability to influence. Information about behavior can become a source of power, prediction, and control.
