SYSTEM [[01 Social Media Contamination]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The continuous intake of information, media, entertainment, narratives, and symbolic content through digital and traditional channels.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms attention into exposure by feeding information into perception, memory, belief formation, and behavioral conditioning.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Social media scrolling  
- Watching videos  
- Reading articles  
- Listening to podcasts  
- News consumption  
- Streaming content  
- Doom scrolling  
- Binge watching

NOTES:  
  
Content consumption is the primary mechanism through which information enters consciousness. The quantity, quality, frequency, and emotional intensity of consumed content influence perception and belief formation.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Most people believe they are choosing content. In reality, content selection is often partially guided by algorithms, habits, social influence, and environmental cues.
