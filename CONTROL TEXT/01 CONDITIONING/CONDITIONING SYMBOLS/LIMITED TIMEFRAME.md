SYSTEM [[02 Existential Deconstruction]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The finite duration available to any living being, process, or experience before change, decline, or termination occurs.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Creates urgency, prioritization, and decision-making by imposing boundaries on available time and opportunity.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Human lifespan  
- Deadlines  
- Aging  
- Seasons  
- Product lifecycles  
- Career duration  
- Historical eras
