DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The simplification, popularization, and mass distribution of psychological concepts, theories, and self-improvement ideas for broad public consumption.

Provides accessible explanations for behavior, emotions, relationships, identity, and personal development through simplified psychological frameworks.

- Self-help content

NOTES:  
  
Pop psychology often translates complex psychological theories into simplified narratives that are easier to understand and apply. While these frameworks can provide useful insights, simplification may omit nuance, context, and scientific limitations.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Psychological frameworks can become identity frameworks. Once a person adopts a psychological label or explanation, it may begin influencing how they interpret themselves and others.
