SYSTEM [[05 Psychological Manipulation]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The reliance on approval, recognition, acceptance, praise, or acknowledgment from external sources to determine self-worth, confidence, or legitimacy.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Influences behavior by linking personal value to the reactions, opinions, and judgments of others.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Social media likes  
- Positive comments  
- Performance reviews  
- Public recognition  
- Awards and achievements  
- Peer approval  
- Follower counts

NOTES:  
  
External validation can provide useful feedback and social connection, but excessive dependence on it may cause behavior to become increasingly shaped by anticipated reactions rather than internal values or intentions.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
When self-worth becomes strongly linked to external approval, influence can be exercised through the distribution, withholding, or manipulation of recognition and acceptance.
