SYSTEM [[01 Social Media Contamination]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The capacity of social platforms, networks, and digital interactions to shape perception, behavior, beliefs, preferences, and decision-making.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transmits ideas, norms, trends, and behaviors across networks through visibility, repetition, social validation, and algorithmic amplification.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Influencer marketing  
- Viral trends  
- Social proof  
- Online activism  
- Brand promotion  
- Political messaging  
- Community-driven behavior

NOTES:  
  
Social media influence operates through networks of attention and social reinforcement. Individuals often adopt beliefs, behaviors, and preferences based on repeated exposure and perceived social consensus.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Influence rarely appears as direct coercion. It is often experienced as voluntary participation, social belonging, or personal preference.
