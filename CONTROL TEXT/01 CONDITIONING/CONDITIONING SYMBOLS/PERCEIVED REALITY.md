NOTES:  
  
Perceived reality is not reality itself, but the model of reality constructed by an individual. This model is continuously updated through experience, information, social interaction, emotion, and belief.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
People respond primarily to their perceived reality rather than objective reality. The interpretation of an event often has greater influence on behavior than the event itself.
