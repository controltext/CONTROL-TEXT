SYSTEM [[03 Economic Control]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The unequal distribution of financial resources, assets, and economic opportunities among individuals, groups, or populations.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Creates differences in access, influence, security, opportunity, and decision-making power through unequal resource allocation.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Income inequality  
- Wealth concentration  
- Billionaire wealth accumulation  
- Generational wealth  
- Unequal access to capital  
- Economic class divisions  
- Asset ownership disparities

NOTES:  
  
Disparity of wealth emerges when resources accumulate unevenly across a population. Economic differences can influence access to education, healthcare, housing, information, and political influence.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Wealth functions as more than a resource. It often translates into increased optionality, resilience, influence, and control over future outcomes.
