SYSTEM [[02 Existential Deconstruction]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The tendency for living organisms to prioritize the preservation of life, security, and continued existence above competing objectives.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Directs attention, behavior, and decision-making toward reducing threats and maintaining survival.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Seeking food and water  
- Avoiding danger  
- Self-preservation  
- Risk assessment  
- Shelter building  
- Emergency response  
- Defensive behavior
