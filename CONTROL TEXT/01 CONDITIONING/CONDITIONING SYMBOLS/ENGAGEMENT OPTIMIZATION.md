SYSTEM [[01 Social Media Contamination]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The deliberate design of content, interfaces, and systems to maximize user interaction, retention, and participation.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Transforms attention into measurable activity by encouraging users to click, view, react, comment, share, or return repeatedly.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Infinite scroll  
- Autoplay videos  
- Push notifications  
- Streak systems  
- Like counters  
- Recommendation loops  
- Personalized feeds

NOTES:  
  
Engagement optimization seeks to maximize interaction by reducing friction and increasing the frequency of user participation.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Systems optimized for engagement do not necessarily optimize for truth, accuracy, well-being, or understanding. They optimize for continued interaction.
