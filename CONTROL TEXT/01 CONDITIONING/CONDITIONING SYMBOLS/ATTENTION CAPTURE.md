SYSTEM [[01 Social Media Contamination]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The process of attracting, directing, and holding human attention through stimuli designed to interrupt, engage, or sustain focus.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Converts awareness into engagement by directing cognitive resources toward a specific object, message, platform, or experience.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Notifications  
- Breaking News Alerts  
- Viral Videos  
- Clickbait Headlines  
- Push Notifications  
- Trending Topics  
- Bright Colors and Visual Cues  
- Emotional Content

NOTES:  
  
Attention is a finite resource. Competing systems seek to attract and retain it because attention precedes perception, engagement, decision-making, and action.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
What captures attention influences what becomes real within subjective experience. Repeated attention reinforces familiarity, significance, and memory.
