SYSTEM [[02 Existential Deconstruction]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The ongoing process through which all forms, structures, identities, and living systems gradually change, deteriorate, transform, or dissolve over time.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Introduces impermanence into existence by ensuring that no condition remains permanently fixed or stable.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Aging  
- Physical deterioration  
- Erosion  
- Entropy  
- Organizational decline  
- Relationship changes  
- The death of stars
