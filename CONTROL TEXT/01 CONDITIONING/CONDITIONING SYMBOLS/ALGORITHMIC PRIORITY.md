SYSTEM [[01 Social Media Contamination]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The process by which automated systems determine the visibility, ranking, and distribution of information.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Controls attention by deciding what information is seen first, seen most often, or not seen at all.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- TikTok For You Page  
- YouTube Recommendations  
- Google Search Rankings  
- Facebook News Feed  
- Instagram Explore Page  
- X (Twitter) Timeline

NOTES:  
  
Algorithmic Priority influences perception indirectly. Rather than forcing a person to think a particular way, it determines which information receives exposure and which information remains invisible. Over time, repeated exposure shapes familiarity, attention, and behavior.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
Visibility is not neutral. Every ranking system contains priorities, whether explicit or implicit.
