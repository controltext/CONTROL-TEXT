SYSTEM [[05 Psychological Manipulation]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
A recurring cycle in which actions, rewards, stimuli, and responses reinforce one another, increasing the likelihood of repeated behavior.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Creates behavioral patterns by linking actions to outcomes that encourage repetition, habit formation, and conditioning.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Social media checking  
- Gambling behavior  
- Video game reward systems  
- Habit formation  
- Notification checking  
- Consumer purchasing cycles  
- Validation seeking
