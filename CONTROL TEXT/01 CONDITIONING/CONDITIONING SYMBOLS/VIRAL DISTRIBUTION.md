NOTES:  
  
Viral distribution increases the speed and scale at which information propagates through a network. Content that spreads virally can influence perception far beyond its original source.  
  
CONTROL TEXT OBSERVATION:  
  
The mechanisms that determine what spreads are often more influential than the content itself. Visibility creates familiarity, and familiarity can create legitimacy.
