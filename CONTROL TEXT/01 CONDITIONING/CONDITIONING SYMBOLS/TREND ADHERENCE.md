SYSTEM [[01 Social Media Contamination]]
DOMAIN [[THE CONDITIONING]]

DEFINITION:  
  
The tendency to adopt behaviors, preferences, opinions, aesthetics, or beliefs because they are perceived as popular, current, or socially validated.  
  
FUNCTION:  
  
Reinforces conformity by encouraging alignment with visible social patterns and emerging cultural movements.  
  
EXAMPLES:  
  
- Viral challenges  
- Fashion trends  
- Popular slang  
- Social media challenges  
- Trending opinions  
- Bandwagon behavior  
- Influencer-driven products
